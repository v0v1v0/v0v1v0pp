get_3d_cross_prod R Documentation

Compute the cross product of two 3D vectors

Description

Compute the cross product of two 3D vectors

Usage

get_3d_cross_prod(v1, v2)

Arguments

v1

First vector, as c(x,y,z)
v2

Second vector, as c(x,y,z)

Value

A vector of length 3 that describes the cross-product

get_3d_cross_prod(v1, v2)
