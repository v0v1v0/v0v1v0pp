GunmaPop2005 R Documentation

Census data of Gunma Prefecture, Japan in 2005

Description

The data gives the populations of Gunma Prefecture, Japan for each age of 0 to 108, males and females separately according to the population census 2005.

Usage

 GunmaPop2005

Format

A data frame with 109 observations and 3 variables.

[, 1] Males numeric Age specific population of males in 2005 Gunma Pref.
[, 2] Females numeric Age specific population of females in 2005 Gunma Pref.
[, 3] Ages numeric Ages from 0 to 108.

Source

http://www.e-stat.go.jp/SG1/estat/List.do?bid=000001005042&amp;cycode=0.
