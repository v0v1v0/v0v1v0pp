kKnot R Documentation

k-Knot

Description

k-Knots of a discrete function.

Usage

kKnot(p, k)

Arguments

p

Vector

k

Degree of the knots

Details

An integer i is a k-knot of p if \Delta^k p(i) >0, where \Delta^k is the k-th Laplacian of the sequence p.

Value

Vector with the k-knots of p.

Author(s)

Jade Giguelay
