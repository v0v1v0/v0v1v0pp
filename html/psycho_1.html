affective R Documentation

Data from the Affective Style Questionnaire (ASQ - French Validation)

Description

This is data from the French validation of the Affective Style Questionnaire.

Usage

affective

Format

A data frame with 1277 rows and 8 variables:

Sex

Sex (F or M)

Birth_Season

Season of birth

Age

Current age

Salary

Salary in euros

Life_Satisfaction

General life satisfaction

Concealing

Concealing score

Adjusting

Adjusting score

Tolerating

Tolerating score
