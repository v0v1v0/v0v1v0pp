Expenditure R Documentation

Household's total expenditure data from Pakistan Social and Living Standards Measurement 2015-16

Description

Expenditure data from Pakistan Social and Living Standards Measurement 2015-16.

Usage

data(Expenditure)

Format

A data.table and data.frame with 24238 observation of 14 variables.

hhcode

Household 10 digits code.

Food

Food and non-alcoholic beverages

Hotels

Restaurants and hotels

Furnishing

Furnishing, Household equipment and routine maintenance of the house

Misc

Miscellaneous goods and services

Tobacco

Alcoholic beverages and tobacco

Housing

Housing, Water, Electricity, Gas and other fuels

Clothing

Clothing and Foot wear

Health

Health

Transport

Transport

Communication

Communication, Postal services

Recreation

Recreation and Culture

Education

Education

DurableGoods

Durable Goods

NonDurable

Total expenditure on non-durable goods
