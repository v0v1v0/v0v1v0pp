wrapper_E_step R Documentation

Wrapper for E step in EM

Description

wrapper_E_step is used in the EM algorithm. It calls itself recursively in case of independent parameters.

Usage

wrapper_E_step(
  phylo,
  times_shared,
  distances_phylo,
  process,
  params_old,
  masque_data,
  F_moments,
  independent,
  Y_data_vec_known,
  miss,
  Y_data,
  U_tree,
  compute_E
)
