PakPC2023PakDist R Documentation

District data from Pakistan Population Census 2023

Description

PakPC2023PakDist Pakistan data from Pakistan Population Census 2023.

Usage

Format

A data.table and data.frame.

Region

Region

Division

Division

District

Pop2023

AHS

Pop2017

Total Population in 2017

GR
