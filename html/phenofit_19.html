cutoff

Description

cutoff

Usage

cutoff(n1, n2, k = n1:n2)

Arguments

n1, n2

peak and trough (or trough and peak)

Value

A numeric vector, in small intervals around (tL + tC)/2 and (tC+tR)/2, respectively, smoothly drop from 1 to 0.
