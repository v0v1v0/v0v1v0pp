dass R Documentation

Dateframe of responses to items from depression, anxiety, and stress scales

Description

The dass data are responses from a random sample of 1,000 individuals collected during the period 2017 â€“ 2019. The data were retrieved July 2020. The items included here are on scales designed to measure depression (14 items), anxiety (13 items), and stress (15 items). The 42 items were presented online and in random order. Respondents were instructed to consider the last week when responding to the items using the following categories: (1) Did not apply to me at all; (2) Applied to me to some degree, or some of the time; (3) Applied to me to a considerable degree, or a good part of the time; (4) Applied to me very much, or most of the time.

Usage

dass
