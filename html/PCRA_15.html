invensysEPS R Documentation

Earnings per Share of Invensys

Description

Yearly earnings-per-share of company Invensys for 17 years. The company's name was invensys prior to 2004.

Usage

data(invensysEPS)

Format

A numeric vector

Source

Corporate Finance Department of Dupont

Examples

library(PCRA)
data(invensysEPS)
invensysEPS
