CRRtoRR R Documentation

Cumulative-risk ratios to risk ratios

Description

Given a vector of cumulative-risk ratios, computes risk ratios

Usage

 CRRtoRR(CRR, DT, h = NULL)

Arguments

CRR

vector of cumulative risk ratios of length m
DT

vector of time increments upon which the cumulative ratios represent. For example if the hazard takes values h_1, h_2,..., h_m on the intervals [t_1, t_2), [t_2, t_3), ..., [t_m, t_{m+1}) then DT will be c(t_2-t_1, t_3 -t_2, ..., t_{m+1} - t_m)

h

The hazard in the reference arm, of length m

Value

The vector of risk ratios at the m time points
