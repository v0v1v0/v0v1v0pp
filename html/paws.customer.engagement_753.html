Description

Update your Amazon SES account VDM attributes.

See https://www.paws-r-sdk.com/docs/sesv2_put_account_vdm_attributes/ for full documentation.

Usage

sesv2_put_account_vdm_attributes(VdmAttributes)

Arguments

VdmAttributes
