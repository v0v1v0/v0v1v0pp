qol R Documentation

Quality of Life

Description

This is quality of life data from the PhenX Quality of Life survey (see https://www.phenxtoolkit.org/protocols/view/221302). This was used by the Clinical Trials Network protocol CTN-0051.

Usage

data(qol)

Format

A tibble with 657 rows and 2 variables:

who
Type: integer
Description: Patient ID
is_homeless
Type: factor (First/Reference level = No)
Description: Are you currently homeless or living in a shelter?
Levels: No, Yes
