navic R Documentation

Phylogeny and pollution sensitivity of diatoms

Description

A phylogenetic tree and the pollution sensitivity of 17 diatoms species from the order Naviculales.

Usage

data(navic)

Format

a phylo4d object.

Source

Keck F., Rimet F., Franc A. & Bouchez A. (In press) Phylogenetic signal in diatom ecology: perspectives for aquatic ecosystems biomonitoring. Ecological Applications.
